Bob Ross

The creator and host of The Joy of Painting

A brief time line of Bob Ross' life:

  • 1942 - Bob Ross was born in Daytona Beach, Florida.
  • 1961 - Enlisted for the US Air Force.
  • 1965 - Bob Ross married his first wife Vivian Ridge.
  • 1977 - Married his wife Jane Ross. Their marriage lasted until her death in 1992.
  • 1981 - Bob retired from the Air Force as a master sergeant. He returned to Florida, studied painting with Bill Alexander, joined his "Alexander Magic Art Supplies Company" and became a traveling salesman and tutor.
  • 1983 - The Joy of Painting's first season was produced. Ross dedicated the first episode of the second season of The Joy of Painting to Bill Alexander.
  • 1990 - The Joy of Painting's 300th episode aired in the United States, and then in Canada. Soon, The Joy of Painting began broadcasting in different countries around the world.
  • 1994 - A few days after being diagnosed with his illness, the final episode aired.
  • 1995 - Bob Ross died from lymphoma at the age of 52.
